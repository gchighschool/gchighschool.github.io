About Our School

Ghonar Chala High School, established on January 10, 1985, is a distinguished non-government secondary school in Sakhipur, Tangail, Bangladesh. Located at Ghonar Chala Bazar, Kachua, the school operates under the Dhaka Education Board with an EIIN of 114612. Officially recognized on January 1, 1988, it offers education in Science, Business Studies, and Humanities. As part of the Monthly Pay Order (MPO) system, it receives government funding for teacher salaries. Situated in a rural plain land area, the school is committed to fostering academic excellence and moral values, supported by dedicated faculty and modern facilities.

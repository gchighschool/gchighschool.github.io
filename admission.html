Admissions

Ghonar Chala High School welcomes applications from students seeking quality education in a nurturing environment. Our admission process is designed to be transparent and accessible, ensuring equal opportunities for all applicants. We offer programs in Science, Business Studies, and Humanities, catering to diverse academic interests.

Admission Process

  • Applications are accepted annually, typically in December, for the upcoming academic year.
  • Prospective students must submit an application form, available at the school office or through our website (when available).
  • Eligibility is based on prior academic performance and an entrance test for certain grades.
  • Selected candidates will be notified, and parents/guardians must complete the enrollment process with required documents.

How to Apply

To apply, visit our school office at Ghonar Chala Bazar, Kachua, Sakhipur, Tangail-1951, or contact us at 01309-114612 for further details. Stay updated on admission deadlines through our official Facebook page.
